Welcome!

Please double check that you have installed the IKBD Injector in your ST as well as the keyboard injection cable from CosmosEx to the ST.

Now you can remote control your ST with this webapp.

  • move your finger on this webpage to move your STs mouse
  • double tap with one finger to left click on the ST
  • double tap with two fingers to doubleclick on the ST
  • right mouse button is not supported yet on touch devices
  • use the ST Keyboard image to type. Just tap the Key you want to enter. You can tap multiple keys (e.g. CTRL ALT DEL for reset if your TOS supports it)
  • hide ST keyboard with "Toggle ST Keyboard"
  • show the touch devices keyboard with "Toggle system keyboard"
  • recieve a screencast from ST (you must have started ce_cast.prg on the ST) by tapping "Start Screencast"

There is now Joystick support in this remote.

ST Screencast

This is a highly experimental feature and will cause instabilities with older software. You will need a bleeding edge browser for this (read: Chrome or Safari). It only works with "clean" ST software, it will not work with games or demos. Do not start ce_cast.prg in your AUTO folder.

Problems? Read this. If this doesn't solve the issue, send a mail!

Welcome!

Please double check that you have installed the IKBD Injector in your ST as well as the keyboard injection cable from CosmosEx to the ST.

Now you can remote control your ST with this webapp.

  • move your mouse on this webpage to move your STs mouse
  • left click in the browser to left click on the ST
  • right click in the browser to right click on the ST
  • click "Grab mouse" (if available in your browser) to attach your mouse to the browser, so you won't leave its boundaries. Press ESC to leave this mode.
  • use your PCs keyboard to type
  • for Special Keys you can click "Toggle ST keyboard" to show a ST Keyboard. Just click the Key you want to enter
  • recieve a screencast from ST (you must have started ce_cast.prg on the ST) by clicking "Start Screencast"

There is now Joystick support in this remote.

ST Screencast

This is a highly experimental feature and will cause instabilities with older software. You will need a bleeding edge browser for this (read: Chromium or Chrome). It only works with "clean" ST software, it will not work with games or demos. It will only work with the most current HDDRIVER. Do not start ce_cast.prg in your AUTO folder.

Problems? Read this. If this doesn't solve the issue, send a mail!

Please use a better browser!
